Outdoor Pool

Offering the luxury of a Balinese swim resort, indulge in the extensive options of five swimming pools designed to suit to your holiday needs. Spend the day in the spacious lagoon-style Main Pool surrounded by tropical gardens or ride down our two water slides in River Pool. Find your peace at our secluded Balinese Feature Pool with emerald green water and cascading waterfalls or at the adult-only Bay Club Pool located in the fitness center. Grand Club pool is exclusively available for our club guests.

Hours:
  1. Grand Club Pool 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, daily
  2. Main Pool 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, daily
  3. Bay Club Pool 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, daily (for adults above 16 years old)
  4. River Pool 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, daily (10:00 AM – 6:00 PM for slides)
  5. Balinese Feature Pool 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, daily
Kids Club

Our Kids Club is designed to take you on an exciting journey of fun and adventure during your holiday. The Kids Club aims to offer an experience above and beyond that will enable the little ones to learn about Balinese culture and exploring the resort’s local flora and fauna in a safe and welcoming setting. Enjoy a variety of fun activities for free and at cost. Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, daily
Kriya Spa

Inspired by the magnificent architecture of an ancient Balinese water palace, the “Wellness Sanctuary” offers the global traveler complete relaxation at the luxurious Kriya Spa at Grand Hyatt Bali Hotel in Indonesia. A team of experienced Spa therapists and aestheticians take time to create programs tailored specifically to one’s lifestyle – ensuring you are taken on a personal journey of tranquility, restoration and reflection. Kriya ‘rituals’ are specially designed for both relaxing and invigorating moments as well as detoxification. Hours: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM, daily Last appointments booked at 7:00 PM Online reservation must be booked a minimum of 24 hours in advance
Fitness Center

Located under the main hotel lobby of Grand Hyatt Bali, the Bay Club Fitness Center offers a wide variety of cardio equipment and machine weights, squash courts and a yoga studio. To refresh after, the center also includes an adults-only swimming pool, sauna, steam room, and plunge pools. A range of classes from yoga to kickboxing are available for free and at cost. Hours: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM, daily

Sustainability Commitment

Along with continuously elevating guest experience, we also make conscious decisions to make your stay more sustainable. We are committed to serving responsibly sourced food, including seafood that are procured strictly from ASC certified suppliers, and eliminated single-use plastic in our hotel. Following Hyatt's World of Care initiatives, we are currently working on waste management, composting and eco-enzyme making.
